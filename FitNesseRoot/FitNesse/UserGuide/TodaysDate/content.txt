To display today's date:

|!c '''Markup Text'''|!c '''Displayed as'''|
|!style_code(!-Today is !today.-!)|Today is !today.|
|!style_code(!-XML is !today -xml.-!)|XML is !today -xml.|
|!style_code[!-Right now it is !today -t.-!]|Right now it is !today -t.|
|!style_code[!-Tomorrow is !today +1.-!]|Tomorrow is !today +1.|
|!style_code[!-Last week was !today -7.-!]|Last week was !today -7.|
|!style_code[!-The month is !today (MMM).-!]|The month is !today (MMM).|
|!style_code[!-The German month is !today -lang=de (MMMM).-!]|The German month is !today -lang=de (MMMM).|
|!style_code[!-The Spanish day of week is !today -lang=es (EEEE).-!]|The Spanish day of week is !today -lang=es (EEEE).|

As you can see, you can add or subtract days.  You can include the time with -t.  And you can use your own format for the time.  (See !style_code(!-SimpleDateFormat-!) for the syntax.)
